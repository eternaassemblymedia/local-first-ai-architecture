# Commercial Waiver Process  
### Cathedral Libraries Archive ¬∑ Aurelia ¬∑ Gatekeeper Architecture  
**Eterna Assembly Media ‚Äî Commercial Access Framework (CAF-1.0)**

This document defines the official process for obtaining a **Commercial Waiver** to use  
the Cathedral + Aurelia + Gatekeeper Architecture in commercial, enterprise, or revenue-bearing environments.

The default license (**DCL-1.0**) prohibits commercial use to protect the integrity, safety, and authorship of the architecture.  
This Commercial Waiver provides the only legal pathway for organizations that wish to:

- integrate the architecture into their products  
- deploy it within internal enterprise systems  
- use it in government, finance, healthcare, or regulated industries  
- build derivative commercial tools or platforms  
- include it in closed-source environments  

This document outlines the steps to request, negotiate, and activate a valid waiver.

Please review these materials:

- `DCL-1.0_LICENSE.md`  
- `GATEKEEPER_ARCHITECTURE_WHITEPAPER.md`  
- `WHY_GATEKEEPER_EXISTS.md`  
- `TECHNICAL_SPECIFICATION.md` (if present)

A Commercial Waiver is only required if your use case involves **any** of the following:

- resale, licensing, or monetization  
- integration into paid products  
- enterprise internal deployment  
- government or institutional use  
- modifications intended for commercial platforms  

If your usage is academic, research, experimental, or personal,  
**you do NOT need a waiver.**

## 3. **Waiver Types**

### **Tier 1 ‚Äî Startup Builder Waiver**
For early-stage teams, indie developers, and small startups.

Allows:
- integration in early commercial prototypes  
- limited user deployment  
- private beta  
- investor demos  

Restrictions:
- must attribute Cathedral/Aurelia as core architecture  
- cannot sublicense the architecture  

### **Tier 2 ‚Äî Enterprise Waiver**
For established companies or large-scale products.

Allows:
- commercial deployment  
- closed-source modifications  
- integration into internal enterprise systems  

Requirements:
- annual licensing fee  
- security/compliance review  
- architectural compatibility verification  

### **Tier 3 ‚Äî Government / Institution Waiver**
Allows usage in:
- public sector  
- regulated industries  
- defense  
- healthcare  
- finance  

Requirements:
- strict compliance handling  
- on-prem isolation  
- optional support contract for audits

## 5. **Decision & Activation**

Upon approval, the applicant receives:

- a signed Commercial Waiver document  
- integration guidelines  
- compliance notes  
- attribution requirements  
- optional support or consultation contracts  

Once signed, the waiver overrides the ‚Äúno commercial use‚Äù clause of DCL-1.0 **only for the approved scope.**

Any usage outside that scope requires a new waiver.

## 6. **Prohibited Uses (Even With Waiver)**

The architecture may **NOT** be used for:

- behavioral surveillance  
- military aggression  
- political manipulation  
- biometric profiling  
- unauthorized user tracking  
- harmful automation  
- systems violating user autonomy  

Violation results in immediate revocation of the waiver and legal follow-up.
